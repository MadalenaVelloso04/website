Personal project 2023

Project Overview

We collaborated with a marketing agency called INKLU to develop a shower gel centered around the theme of “making off glam.” Our goal was to emphasize transparency, inclusivity, and naturality throughout the design and concept. This shower gel represented a partnership between INKLU, us, and Vogue Portugal, combining our shared vision for a product that celebrates authentic beauty and embraces diverse identities.
Inclusivity became the heartbeat of our philosophy. We celebrated all genders and championed the idea of beauty without makeup, recognizing and valuing the rich diversity of personalities and tastes that exist in the world.

Concept and Creation

As we developed our shower gel, we infused it with unique characteristics that set it apart. The cap design embodied our slogan, “making off glam.” It symbolized the moment of removing a smooth-surfaced cap to reveal the imperfect texture of the bottle underneath, capturing the essence of embracing one's natural self.
The textural detail of the bottle added depth and character to our product. The curved cut-out on the cap distinguished our shower gel from conventional designs, transforming it into not just a product, but a memorable experience that resonated with our core values.
