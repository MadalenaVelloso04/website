LETS TALK
My name is Madalena Velloso, and I'm a young Portuguese student designer with a deep-rooted passion for fine arts. Growing up immersed in a world of culture and visual communication, with parents in the history of art field, I've always been surrounded by creativity and artistic expression.
In this portfolio, you'll find a glimpse into some of my projects over the years, ranging from branding to 3D animation, and much more. My work is a blend of traditional fine arts and modern design, allowing me to explore creative solutions with freedom and depth.
The combination of my fine arts background with my design studies has pushed me to think beyond the conventional, always returning to the essentials—paper and pencil—as the foundation of my creative process. I believe that merging classical techniques with digital innovation leads to unique and impactful designs.
